About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Université Laval and MILA – Québec AI Institute. My research lies at the intersection of interactive learning, statistical learning, and automated machine learning. I'm particularly interested in building robust, efficient, and generalizable ML systems, with a strong emphasis on theoretical foundations and practical applications.
